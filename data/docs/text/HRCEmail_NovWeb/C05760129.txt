I. Committee Nomination Status




The Advisory Committee on Persons with Disabilities has three vacancies at this time. Additionally, taking into
consideration the recommendations of committee Executive Director, Stephanie Ortoleva, we have conducted an
evaluation of existing committee members, and feel that reorganization is warranted, opening an additional two
vacancies for a total of five slots.
